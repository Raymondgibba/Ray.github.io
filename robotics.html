Introduction to Robotics

Robotics is the integration of hardware and software to create intelligent machines that can assist in automation, problem-solving, and human interactions. From industrial robots to AI-driven assistants, robotics is shaping the future.

Future of Robotics

Robotics is evolving rapidly, with advancements in AI, IoT, and automation. In the future, we can expect:

  • Fully autonomous robots for space exploration.
  • AI-powered robots in healthcare and education.
