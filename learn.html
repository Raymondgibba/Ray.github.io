About Me

Ever since I was young, I have been fascinated by **technology, robotics, and problem-solving**. My journey started as a child in Kilchering, The Gambia, where I found myself dismantling gadgets just to understand how they worked. Today, I am a **robotics educator, Arduino expert, and content creator**, helping to shape the next generation of tech enthusiasts.

I currently serve as a student and a student trainer at Sky Lab, where I train students in robotics and prepare them for the **FIRST Global Challenge**. My work involves not only teaching technical skills but also fostering teamwork and problem-solving abilities.

Beyond robotics, I am also passionate about **web development, graphic design, and automation projects**, constantly pushing the boundaries of innovation.
