My Research Journey

Exploring Innovations in Robotics, Automation, and Smart Technologies

Introduction

Research is at the heart of innovation, and I have dedicated my time to exploring various areas of technology, including robotics, smart automation, and sustainability. Through these projects, I have tackled real-world problems and designed solutions that make an impact.

1. Smart Pet Feeder and Water Dispenser

Objective: To create an automated system that feeds pets based on their presence and refills their water when necessary.

Challenges Faced:

  • Setting accurate ultrasonic sensor thresholds to detect pets without false triggers.
  • Ensuring the water level sensor worked consistently without malfunction.

Solution:

  • Optimized the ultrasonic sensor code to detect only close-range objects.
  • Implemented a real-time feedback system to check water levels before activating the pump.

2. Smart Trash Can

Objective: To develop an intelligent trash bin that opens automatically when approached.

Challenges Faced:

  • Calibrating the servo motor for smooth lid movement.
  • Minimizing power consumption to increase battery efficiency.

Solution:

  • Used a delay mechanism to ensure the lid remained open long enough before closing.
  • Added a sleep mode to reduce power usage when not in use.

3. Fire Sensor System

Objective: To build a fire detection system using an Arduino and flame sensor.

Challenges Faced:

  • Ensuring the sensor detected fire without reacting to regular light sources.
  • Triggering an alert system quickly enough for emergency response.

Solution:

  • Fine-tuned the sensor sensitivity to differentiate between normal light and flames.
  • Integrated a buzzer and an LED indicator for instant alerts.

4. Water Level Alert System

Objective: To create an alert system that notifies when a water tank is almost full.

Challenges Faced:

  • Ensuring the sensor accurately detected different water levels.
  • Preventing false alarms caused by water turbulence.

Solution:

  • Positioned the sensor at an optimal level to detect the final threshold only.
  • Implemented a delay function to reduce sensitivity to minor fluctuations.

5. Lettuce Production Research

Objective: To analyze and improve lettuce farming techniques in Brikama.

Challenges Faced:

  • Finding reliable data on local production rates.
  • Identifying factors affecting crop yield and sustainability.

Solution:

  • Conducted field studies to gather data from local farmers.
  • Proposed smart irrigation and nutrient management solutions.

Future Research Goals

My journey in research and innovation continues. In the future, I plan to explore:

  • AI integration into robotics for enhanced automation.
  • Smart composting systems to improve waste management.
  • Renewable energy solutions for sustainable farming.
Research is an ongoing process, and I am excited to push the boundaries of technology to create meaningful solutions.
