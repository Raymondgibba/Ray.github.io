About My Training

I am Raymond Gibba, a passionate robotics trainer dedicated to equipping students with hands-on skills in **robotics, embedded systems, and automation**. My training is focused on **practical learning**, allowing students to build real projects that help them understand the principles of technology.

Through my sessions, students learn how to **design, program, and control** robots using different platforms, preparing them for competitions like the **FIRST Global Challenge** and real-world applications. Whether it's learning microcontroller programming with **Arduino**, competing with **VEX and REV Robotics**, or diving into AI-driven automation, my training ensures that students gain the confidence and expertise needed to excel.

What My Training Covers:

  • Hands-on Arduino Projects: Learn to build smart systems like **automated pet feeders, fire detection systems, and irrigation systems**.
  • REV Robotics & VEX Robotics: Advanced robotics training for competitions and engineering projects.
  • Embedded Systems: Understand how **sensors, motors, and microcontrollers** work together to create intelligent systems.
  • AI & Automation: Explore how artificial intelligence is used in robotics and smart automation.
  • Team-Based Learning: Encouraging collaboration, problem-solving, and creativity in technology.
